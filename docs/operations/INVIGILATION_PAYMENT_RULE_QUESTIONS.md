# Invigilation Payment Rule Questions

**Date**: 2026-06-02  
**Audience**: Admin, finance, academic office, exam operations owner

These questions must be answered before EMS implements or claims final invigilation payment calculation.

## 1. Payment Unit

- Is payment per exam session?
- Is payment per hour?
- Is payment per day?
- Is another unit used?
- How is partial attendance handled?

## 2. Rate

- Is the rate the same for all assigned people?
- Does the rate differ by duty role?
- Does the rate differ for staff, lecturer, and external invigilator?
- Are rates different for internal and external exams?
- Who owns rate changes?

## 3. Duty Roles

- Chief invigilator
- Assistant invigilator
- Staff runner
- Exam paper distribution
- Room opening or room operation staff
- Print shop staff
- Other roles

For each role, confirm whether it is payable in EMS.

## 4. Evidence

- Is schedule assignment enough?
- Is check-in required?
- Is a signature required?
- Is QR confirmation required?
- Is supervisor approval required?
- Is paper form evidence still required?
- What evidence is sufficient for external invigilators?

## 5. Exceptions

- No-show
- Late arrival
- Replacement
- Cancelled exam
- Room change
- Merged rooms
- Split sections
- Emergency duty assignment
- Duplicate assignment

For each exception, confirm whether payment is kept, reduced, cancelled, or transferred.

## 6. Approval

- Who verifies the duty evidence?
- Who approves payment batches?
- Who can reopen or correct a payment batch?
- Who exports the payment report?
- Is DPO or audit review required before export?

## 7. Output

- Excel payment report?
- PDF approval sheet?
- Person-level summary?
- Exception report?
- Audit trail export?
- Integration to finance system?
- Required finance column names and formats?

## 8. Current EMS Compatibility

- Should existing `supervisions.compensation` remain the storage field?
- Should `export_compensation` be renamed in UI/docs to invigilation payment export?
- Are current default rates such as 200/300 valid, placeholders, or obsolete?
- Should external exam compensation be included in the same payment batch?
